170A Community Projects & Digital Resources

A curated list of high-value external projects, podcasts, and channels relevant created by the 170A community.

GingerHacker (YouTube & Initiative)

A leading resource for accessible cybersecurity education, focusing on tutorials, career guidance, and community mentorship. The channel features walkthroughs, career advice, and deep dives into tools and concepts relevant to both entry-level and experienced cyber professionals, including veterans and career changers.

Resources:

  • GingerHacker YouTube Channel (Video content & tutorials)
  • The Ginger Hacker Initiative (Nonprofit with mentorship, scholarships, and resources)

Hacked By Halftime (Podcast)

A popular podcast focused on cyber operations, incident response, and the tactical use of cyber within a military or strategic context. It provides valuable insight into real-world applications of cyber strategy and technical implementation.
