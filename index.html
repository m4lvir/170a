Welcome to the 170A Wolfpack

This site is designed to be an unofficial resource for potential 170As and 170As (Cyber Warfare Technician). Downloads will have WOCS, WOBC, and WOAC related pdfs. Projects will feature projects created by 170As, and Professional Development will have resources for advancing as a 170A and for civilian life. Technical Developemnt will have resources for learning new skills and passing certifications. Contact has an email that you can use to ask questions and for an invite to the 170A signal chat.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Pre-Warrant Selection Board

Q: Can you look at my packet?

A: We can look at your packet after it is at least 90% done. You should use U.S. Army Recruiting Command for official documents.

Warrant Officer Candidate School

Q: Does Active Duty need a 1610 to go to WOCS?

A: No.

Q: What is a DA 2-1?

A: It is a Personanel Qualification Record. Active Duty does not need to worry about having a DA 2-1.

Q: What is needed for WOCS?

A: Downloads has the WOCS Orientation pdf which has a packing list. It is a good idea to bring extras of items like medical tape.

Q: What is the most difficult part of WOCS?

A: Land navigation has the highest failure rate.

170A Warrant Officer Basic Course

Q: Can you graduate early?

A: If you have already taken CCTC, you can skip WOBC's CCTC and graduate early.

Q: Do you need a 1610?

A: If you are stationed at Fort Gordon, you don't need a 1610.

170A Warrant Officer Advance Course

Q: What can I do to prepare for WOAC?

A: There is a reading list for WOAC that can be found in Downloads.
